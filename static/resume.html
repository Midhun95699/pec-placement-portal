✅ ATS Checklist
🚀 How to Beat ATS
→Use the exact job title in your summary (e.g. "Software Engineer" not "SE")
→Add keywords from the job description verbatim into skills and experience
→Avoid tables, headers/footers, and images — ATS parsers can't read them
→Use standard section headings: "Experience", "Education", "Skills"
→Use bullet points with action verbs: "Developed", "Implemented", "Optimized"
→Spell out acronyms at least once: "Machine Learning (ML)"
→Include LinkedIn & GitHub — many ATS systems rank these profiles
→Keep formatting clean: standard fonts, no columns (single column is safer)
→Quantify achievements: "Improved load time by 40%", "Managed team of 5"
